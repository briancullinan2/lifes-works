Introduction

Jazz is an evolving repertory of musical material that is played on musical instruments and sung in a number of different musical styles by various performers for differing purposes since the early part of the twentieth century. Improvisation, or spontaneously creating music in the moment, is often considered the highest expression in jazz.

Jazz is a hybrid mix of evolving and changing music styles, including

Cut 'n' Mix
Fusion
Modal
Bebop
Swing
Dixieland

We will discuss in detail each of these musical styles in the middle portion of the course.

Musical styles
Musical styles in jazz are unified by a common approach to performance practice, instrumentation, or textual concerns.
