Miles and his bands (late 1960s to early 1970s)

Introduction

If you need to review the article on Miles Davis, please click on link.

Miles Davis, so characteristically, was not the first to fuse jazz with rock or funk. Experiments by Gary Burton, Soft Machine, and Miles' own late 1960s drummer Tony Williams' group Lifetime were recording jazz fusion before Miles, but Miles certainly took the new trend and did remarkable things with it.
