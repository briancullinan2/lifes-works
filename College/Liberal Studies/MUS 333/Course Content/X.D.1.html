Louis Armstrong and His Hot Five

We first heard Louis Armstrong and His Hot Five earlier in the ‘Dixieland to Swing’ section of the course. They were the hottest band on record and only appeared together in the studio. The front line most often consisted of the best in the business: Jimmy Dodds on clarinet, Kid Ory on trombone, and anchored by Armstrong on cornet (and then later on trumpet). The second line was no less distinguished with Lil Hardin Armstrong on piano and Johnny St. Cyr on banjo.
