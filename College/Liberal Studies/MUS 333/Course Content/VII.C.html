Trends that would influence cool

There were other musicians working in swing that would profoundly shape the development of cool. Probably the single most influential swing musician for cool has to be tenor sax great, Lester Young.

Lester Young

Read the article on Lester Young.

Amid the screaming energy of Count Basie’s Kansas City style swing big band, Lester Young sat in the middle and barely broke a sweat. He put the ‘c’ in cool.

Follow the chart below and listen to Count Basie’s Kansas City Seven’s “Lester Leaps In” (1939).
