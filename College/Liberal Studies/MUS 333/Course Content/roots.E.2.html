Musical Form and Blues

There are a variety of musical forms that are called blues, from 8 bar blues to dance forms of 16 and 32 bars. All include a distinctive harmonic pattern of a given length that animates two or more lines of lyrics, if it is sung. For jazz, the most common form of blues is 12 bar blues.
